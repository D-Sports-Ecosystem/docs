## Policy baseline

- Apple App Store Review Guidelines `3.1.1`: disclose randomized virtual-item probabilities.
- Google Play policy: provide clear randomized-item disclosure near transaction/opening surfaces.
- Regional disclosure enforcement context (including Korea/China) informs stricter disclosure discipline.

## Ratings and descriptor consistency

- ESRB/PEGI random-item descriptor language should remain consistent with in-app disclosure copy.
- In-app wording must avoid deterministic claims unless a backend guarantee explicitly exists.

## Required implementation outcomes

- Odds are presented from backend payloads, not hardcoded.
- Disclosure is visible or one-tap accessible close to purchase/open actions.
- Failure states provide user-actionable, non-misleading feedback.

## Disallowed patterns

- Implied guaranteed rarity without explicit backend guarantee.
- Marketing language that overstates random-outcome certainty.
- Hidden or hard-to-reach disclosure UI paths.
