- `ModerationReport`
- `ReportType`
- `ReportStatus`

## High-level flow

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  userSubmission[UserSubmission] --> intakeRoute[FeedbackOrReportRoute]
  intakeRoute --> reportWrite[ModerationReportWrite]
  reportWrite --> statusLifecycle[ReportStatusLifecycle]
  statusLifecycle --> downstreamReview[PrivilegedReviewSystems]
```

## Architectural notes

- Non-admin routes focus on safe intake and structured report persistence.
- Review, escalation, and resolution actions are downstream and generally privileged.
- Feedback intake and moderation-report entities are related but may originate from separate route families.
