## Feature parity with backend/PWA

`d-sports-engage-native` targets high parity with `d-sports-api` feature behavior while optimizing for mobile UX patterns.

## Wallet

- **Tokens and holdings** — Fetches balances and token metadata from `wallet-api`. Displays token details with brand color mapping (`lib/token-utils.ts`).
- **Pack opening** — Full pack-open flow with animated reveal (`PackOpeningModal`), inventory refresh, and status tracking.
- **Crypto checkout** — Thirdweb SDK signs on-chain transactions (Arbitrum, Ethereum, Polygon) and calls the PWA backend at `POST /api/checkout/crypto` + `POST /api/checkout/crypto/verify`.
- **Fiat checkout** — RevenueCat handles Apple IAP (iOS), Google Play (Android), and Stripe (web).

## Shop

- **Collectibles** — Grid of collectible cards with rarity-based styling (`lib/rarity-utils.ts`). Detail modals include 3D GLB viewers (Filament on native, Three.js on web).
- **Cart** — Multi-item cart with coin bundles and collectible items managed in the Zustand store.
- **Coin bundles** — In-app purchase bundles via RevenueCat with a dedicated `CoinBundleModal`.

## Locker room

- **Social feed** — Post creation, reactions, comments, and UGC moderation (hide/report). Logic in `hooks/use-feed-section.ts`.
- **Daily games** — Pick 'Em, Spin Wheel, and Guess the Player with dedicated game components.
- **Quests** — Team-scoped quest progression, pass-gated eligibility, and reward claim flows. Quest detail modals show completion criteria and rewards.
- **Explore** — Browse fans and teams with search and follow interactions.

## Leaderboard

- **Global and team-scoped** rankings with configurable filters.
- `BaseLeaderboard` component handles pagination, styling, and modal expansion.

## Profile

- **User profile** — Avatar, bio, badges, achievements, points history, followers/following.
- **Achievements** — Badge images cached via `CachedBadgeImage`, resolved through `lib/resolve-badge-image-uri.ts`.
- **Other user profiles** — `app/profile/[handle].tsx` for viewing other users.

## Settings

- **Account** — Connected accounts, security, blocked users, account deletion.
- **Appearance** — Theme toggle (dark/light).
- **Accessibility** — Font size, motion reduction, screen reader optimizations. Synced via `AccessibilitySettingsSync` component.
- **Notifications** — Push notification preferences (OneSignal).
- **Support** — Contact support, report a problem, help center.
- **Legal** — EULA, privacy policy, terms of service (`constants/eula.ts`, `constants/privacy-policy.ts`, `constants/terms-of-service.ts`).
- **Changelog** — In-app changelog rendered from `constants/changelog-data.ts`.

## Team-aware experiences

- Team discovery, favorite-team interactions, and team detail modals are integrated across locker room and profile surfaces.
- Team and league metadata is sourced from backend sync pipelines fed by the `leagues` repository.
- `ExploreTeams` component provides search, filtering, and team follow interactions.
