---
title: "Odds disclosure copy guidelines"
description: "Approved copy and accessibility guidance for native pack-odds disclosure UI."
---

## Required copy blocks

- `Drop Rates (per card)`
- Rarity + percentage rows (for example, `Common: 50.00%`)
- Optional guarantee line (only when backend provides one)
- Optional expected outcomes summary

## Tone and clarity rules

- Clearly state outcomes are probability-based and random.
- Avoid deterministic language unless explicit guarantee exists.
- Keep wording concise and screenshot-friendly for app-review artifacts.

## Accessibility rules

- Maintain strong contrast in dark and light themes.
- Avoid multi-step navigation to reveal disclosure.
- Use clear toggle labels (`Show` / `Hide`).

## Disallowed phrases

- `Guaranteed legendary` without backend guarantee.
- `Always high rarity`.
- Value claims that imply certainty for random outcomes.

## Policy alignment

- Keep disclosure placement aligned with Apple and Google randomized-item expectations.
- Keep ratings descriptor language consistent with in-app disclosure behavior.
