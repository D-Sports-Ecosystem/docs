## Scope

This section documents client-facing compliance requirements for the native app, including pack-opening disclosure, user-generated content (UGC) moderation safeguards, and legal/terms gating.

## Included topics

- Pack odds integration from backend payloads
- Disclosure copy and accessibility constraints
- UGC report/block moderation and EULA acceptance gating
- App-review evidence requirements for release submissions

## Public-safe boundary

- Included: implementation expectations and review-ready artifact templates.
- Excluded: private legal strategy or internal-only escalation channels.
